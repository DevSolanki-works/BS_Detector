SAMPLE RENTAL AGREEMENT — CLAUSE EXCERPT
Vadodara, Gujarat

CLAUSE 7 — SECURITY DEPOSIT AND DEDUCTIONS
The Tenant shall deposit a sum equivalent to three (3) months' rent as security deposit
prior to possession. The Landlord shall have the absolute right to deduct from the security
deposit any amounts deemed necessary for repairs, cleaning, unpaid rent, or any other
charges at the Landlord's sole discretion, without prior notice or itemized accounting to
the Tenant. The remaining balance, if any, shall be returned within 90 (ninety) days of
vacating the premises.

CLAUSE 11 — TERMINATION BY LANDLORD
The Landlord reserves the right to terminate this agreement with 7 (seven) days written
notice for any reason, including but not limited to personal use of the property, sale of
the property, or at the Landlord's sole discretion. The Tenant waives any right to contest
such termination in a court of law and agrees that the Landlord's decision shall be final
and binding.

CLAUSE 15 — MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
All maintenance and repairs, including structural repairs, electrical, and plumbing, shall
be the sole responsibility of the Tenant unless such damage exceeds Rs. 50,000, in which
case the Landlord may, at his discretion, contribute to the repair costs. The Tenant shall
not make any alterations to the property without written consent, and any improvements
made by the Tenant shall become the permanent property of the Landlord without compensation.
